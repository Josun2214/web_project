I want to have my laundry done.

Place all laundry into one of the bags provided on the reception counter (5,000 won. Both washing/drying are included). Place the laundry fee in the bag on the right. Laundry will be completed within 24 hours and can be collected from in front of the reception counter.

When does the main door close?

The main door is open 24hrs a day.
